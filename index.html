About

Born and raised in the Denver, CO area. I had exposure to tattoos at the ripe age of 18 and this opened a door that will never be closed. I've always loved art and all the different types of media and expressions to be done with it. I've been tattooing since 2005 and loving every minute of it. I enjoy all forms and styles, but really prefer working in full color, and like to doing new school styled tattoos. No project is too big or too small, I love doing them all.
